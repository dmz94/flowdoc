    What is Decant?

    Decant strips away clutter from web articles and reformats them with typography designed for readers with dyslexia. It is also useful for anyone who prefers cleaner, distraction-free reading.

    It works on any device, but you'll get the best experience on a desktop or tablet.

    Two ways to use Decant

    Paste a URL -- for any public web page. Paste the address into the input bar and press Convert.

    Upload a file -- for pages behind logins, paywalls, or apps. Save the page from your browser (File > Save As > HTML), then drag the file onto Decant or click Browse.

    Make it yours

    Every reader is different. These controls let you adjust the page until it works for you.

    • Aa -- Font, size, content width, and spacing. Wider spacing and larger text help many dyslexic readers. Use Reset to restore defaults.
    • Theme -- Light, Sepia, Dark, or High Contrast. Some readers find lower contrast or dark backgrounds easier on the eyes.

    Your choices are saved automatically -- set it up once and it stays that way on your next visit.

    Save and share

    • Save -- Print or download the converted page. Downloaded files work offline and can be emailed, shared, or printed later. A dedicated PDF export is coming soon -- for now, choose Print and select "Save as PDF" from your printer options.
    • Share -- Copies a special link to your clipboard. When someone opens it, they see the article already converted -- no extra steps. Available for URL conversions only.
    • View Original -- Opens the original page in a new tab so you can see the difference. Available for URL conversions only.

    Quick access

    On a phone or tablet, you can add Decant to your home screen for quick access. In Chrome, tap the menu and select 'Add to Home Screen.' In Safari, tap the share button and select 'Add to Home Screen.'
